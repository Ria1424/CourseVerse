Subscription FAQs

How does the AI Tutor work?

Our AI Study Companion reads the lesson transcripts, summaries, and code blocks for the course you are currently studying. It gives precise, tailored explanations rather than generic chat responses.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can upgrade and downgrade your subscription tier instantly. There are no locking contracts, and you can switch back to the Free plan at any time.

Are the certificates verified?

Every certificate earned has a unique credential tag tracked in our database. When you complete a course, it automatically populates on your dashboard, showing your achievement level and date.
